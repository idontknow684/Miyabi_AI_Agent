## Red Flags & Warning Signs

### Critical Issues (Immediate Action Needed)

| Warning Sign | Threshold | Action |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| **Low Day 1 views** | <500 | Increase promotion immediately |
| **Poor CTR** | <4% | Change thumbnail/title ASAP |
| **High drop-off (0-30s)** | >30% | Analyze hook, consider re-upload with new intro |
| **Very low engagement** | <1% | Check if promoted to wrong audience |
| **Negative comments** | >20% of comments | Address concerns, clarify messaging |

### Moderate Issues (Monitor & Optimize)

| Warning Sign | Threshold | Action |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| **Slow growth** | <50% of targets | Increase organic promotion, analyze what's not working |
| **Low search traffic** | <10% | Improve SEO (title, description, tags) |
| **Low suggested traffic** | <30% | Improve retention, CTR |
| **Geographic mismatch** | <50% target audience | Adjust targeting in paid ads |

## Success Scenarios & Actions

### Scenario A: Viral Success (200K+ views)

**Actions**:
1. Prepare for high traffic (server capacity)
2. Have investor materials ready
3. Fast-track follow-up content
4. Reach out to press proactively
5. Scale paid promotion
6. Hire help for community management

### Scenario B: Target Achieved (100K-200K views)

**Actions**:
1. Continue steady promotion
2. Analyze what worked, replicate
3. Plan content series
4. Reach out to warm investor leads
5. Build community

### Scenario C: Good Performance (50K-100K views)

**Actions**:
1. Identify gaps (CTR? Retention? Promotion?)
2. Optimize based on data
3. Create follow-up content addressing feedback
4. Double down on best-performing channels
5. Extend promotion timeline

### Scenario D: Underperformance (<50K views)

**Actions**:
1. Deep analysis of why (thumbnail? title? content? timing?)
2. Consider re-upload with improvements
3. Create complementary content (blog post, Twitter thread)
4. Reach out to influencers for help
5. Learn lessons for next video
