## Revenue Model

### Pricing Tiers (Annual Contract Value)

#### **Free Tier** (Developer Adoption)
- **Price**: $0
- **Target**: Individual developers, OSS projects
- **Limits**:
  - 50 Issues/month
  - 1 concurrent agent
  - Community support
- **Goal**: Build 100K+ user base for virality

#### **Team Tier** (SMB Sweet Spot)
- **Price**: $99/seat/month ($1,188/year)
- **Target**: 5-50 developer teams
- **Includes**:
  - Unlimited issues
  - 5 concurrent agents
  - Advanced analytics
  - Slack/Discord integration
  - Email support (24h SLA)
- **Goal**: 1,000 teams by Q4 2026

#### **Enterprise Tier** (High-Value Accounts)
- **Price**: $299/seat/month ($3,588/year)
- **Target**: Fortune 500, Japanese IT giants (NTT, Hitachi, Fujitsu)
- **Includes**:
  - Unlimited everything
  - Unlimited concurrent agents
  - SSO/SAML
  - VOICEVOX narration (Japan-specific)
  - Dedicated CSM
  - 99.9% SLA
  - Custom agent development
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Premium support (4h SLA)
- **Goal**: 10 enterprise accounts by Q2 2026 (avg $500K ACV)

#### **Enterprise+ Tier** (Strategic Accounts)
- **Price**: Custom ($1M+ ACV)
- **Target**: KDDI, AWS, GitHub partnerships
- **Includes**:
  - White-label licensing
  - Source code access
  - Co-marketing rights
  - Joint roadmap planning
  - Dedicated engineering resources

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Q1 2026)

**Objective**: Establish market credibility and beta customer pipeline

1. **Research Publication Blitz**
   - Publish 2 papers at top-tier conferences (ICSE, FSE)
   - Present at NeurIPS 2026 (AI track)
   - Leverage Shunsuke Hayashi's "Law of Flickering Scenery" paper

2. **Beta Program Launch**
   - Target: 50 invite-only companies
   - Focus: Japanese enterprises (cultural fit) + US tech companies
   - Success metrics: 80% satisfaction, 5+ case studies

3. **Community Building**
   - Launch Discord community (target: 5K members)
   - Weekly "Miyabi Office Hours" (live Q&A)
   - Create YouTube series: "Autonomous Development 101"

### Phase 2: Market Entry (Q2-Q3 2026)

**Objective**: Drive initial revenue and product-market fit

1. **Freemium Launch**
   - Public beta: Open registration
   - Goal: 10K free users in 3 months
   - Conversion target: 2% to paid (200 Team tier)

2. **Enterprise PoC Program**
   - Target: 20 enterprise pilots
   - Free 2-month PoC
   - Success criteria: 50% cost reduction, 3x throughput
   - Win rate goal: 50% (10 paid contracts)

3. **Partnership Development**
   - **AWS**: Marketplace listing, co-selling
   - **GitHub**: Enterprise integration, co-marketing
   - **KDDI/NTT**: Japan market entry, telco partnerships

### Phase 3: Scale (Q4 2026 - Q4 2027)

**Objective**: Scale to $37M ARR with repeatable sales motion

1. **Sales Team Build-Out**
   - Hire VP of Sales (Q4 2026)
   - Build 10-person SDR team
   - Build 10-person AE team
   - Build 5-person SE team

2. **Geographic Expansion**
   - Japan: Tokyo office (Q1 2027)
   - US: SF Bay Area office (Q2 2027)
   - Europe: London office (Q4 2027)

3. **Product-Led Growth**
   - Self-serve Team tier signup
   - Usage-based expansion
   - Land-and-expand playbook
