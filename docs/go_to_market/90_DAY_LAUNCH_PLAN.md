### What Worked ‚úÖ

1. **Product-Market Fit**: Customers love Miyabi (NPS 85)

3. **Beta ‚Üí Paid Conversion**: 3/50 beta customers converted (6% - higher than expected)

4. **Customer Success**: Need CSM team (hired 1, need 3 by Q2)
