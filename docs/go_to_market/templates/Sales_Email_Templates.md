## Best Practices

### Email Writing Tips

**Subject Lines**:
‚úÖ DO: Personalize with company name, numbers, results
‚úÖ DO: Keep under 50 characters (mobile optimization)
‚úÖ DO: Use curiosity ("Quick question about [Company]")
‚ùå DON'T: Use "Re:" unless it's a real reply
‚ùå DON'T: Use all caps, excessive punctuation
‚ùå DON'T: Use generic subjects ("Following up")

**Body**:
‚úÖ DO: Keep under 150 words (short, scannable)
‚úÖ DO: Use bullet points (easy to skim)
‚úÖ DO: Include social proof (customer names, metrics)
‚úÖ DO: Have ONE clear call-to-action
‚úÖ DO: Add a P.S. (most-read part of emails)
‚ùå DON'T: Send walls of text
‚ùå DON'T: Use jargon, buzzwords
‚ùå DON'T: Sound robotic (be human!)

**Timing**:
‚úÖ Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 2-4pm (recipient's timezone)
‚úÖ Avoid Monday mornings (inbox overload)
‚úÖ Avoid Friday afternoons (mentally checked out)
‚úÖ Follow up 3 times (Day 1, Day 5, Day 12)

**Personalization**:
‚úÖ Use first name (never "Hi there" or "Dear Sir/Madam")
‚úÖ Reference trigger events (funding, hiring, product launches)
‚úÖ Mention mutual connections (LinkedIn, investors)
‚úÖ Research their GitHub, blog posts, tweets
