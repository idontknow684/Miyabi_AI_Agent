## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Visual Style

**Colors**:
- Primary: Deep Blue (#0A2463) - Trust, technology
- Secondary: Vibrant Green (#3BBA9C) - Growth, success
- Accent: Warm Orange (#F26419) - Energy, innovation
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray (#F5F5F5)

**Typography**:
- Headings: Inter Bold or SF Pro Display (60pt+)
- Body: Inter Regular or SF Pro Text (24-32pt)
- Captions: Inter Light (18pt)

**Imagery**:
- Product screenshots: High-res, annotated
- Icons: Line-style (Feather Icons or Heroicons)
- Charts: Clean, minimal (Apple Keynote style)
- Photos: Professional headshots, aspirational team images

### Dos and Don'ts

**DO**:
- Use large fonts (investors read from distance or small screens)
- One idea per slide (don't cram)
- Show > Tell (charts > bullet points)
- Use real numbers (not "significant" or "substantial")
- Cite sources (builds credibility)

**DON'T**:
- Use more than 3 colors per slide
- Include walls of text (max 3 bullet points)
- Use clip art or cheesy stock photos
- Animate excessively (subtle fades only)
- Forget page numbers (investors: "Go back to slide 8")
