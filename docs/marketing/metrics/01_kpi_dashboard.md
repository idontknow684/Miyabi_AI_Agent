## Red Flags & Action Triggers

### Trigger 1: Declining Growth
**Definition:** 2 consecutive weeks of negative growth in key metric

**Action:**
1. Deep-dive analysis (why?)
2. Survey community (feedback)
3. Adjust content strategy
4. Consider paid promotion

### Trigger 2: Low Engagement
**Definition:** Engagement rate drops below 2% (Twitter) or 10% DAU (Discord)

**Action:**
1. Increase interaction (more replies, AMAs)
2. Launch engagement campaign (challenge, hackathon)
3. Audit content quality

### Trigger 3: Pipeline Stagnation
**Definition:** No new qualified leads for 2 consecutive weeks

**Action:**
1. Review lead generation tactics
2. Increase outbound (LinkedIn, email)
3. Host webinar/event
4. Create enterprise-focused content

### Trigger 4: Churn
**Definition:** Discord members leaving >5%/month OR customer churn >3%/month

**Action:**
1. Exit interviews (why leaving?)
2. Improve product/community
3. Increase engagement
4. Address major pain points
