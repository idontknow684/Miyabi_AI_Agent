## Pitch Strategy

### The Hook Formula

**Successful tech PR pitches follow this structure:**
1. **Surprising stat** (94.7% vs. 78%)
2. **Big problem** (developer shortage, unreliable AI)
3. **Novel solution** (mathematical proofs)
4. **Validation** (research papers, traction)
5. **Human story** (solo founder, contrarian approach)

### What Journalists Want

**Data:**
- Specific numbers (not "very fast"â€”say "2.78Ã— faster")
- Comparative data (vs. AutoGPT, vs. Devin)
- Market size ($180B software development market)

**Exclusivity:**
- Offer 24-48 hour exclusives to Tier 1 outlets
- Embargo period (for coordinated launch)
- First look at new data/features

**Access:**
- Interview with founder
- Demo accounts
- Early access to products
- Behind-the-scenes content

**Visuals:**
- Screenshots, diagrams, videos
- Not just text press releases
- Social-media-ready assets

### Timing

**Best Days:**
- **Tuesday-Thursday** (avoid Monday crush, Friday news dumps)

**Best Times:**
- **6-9am PT** (East Coast journalists starting day)

**Avoid:**
- Holidays, major events (WWDC, AWS re:Invent)
- Competitor launch days
- US election weeks
