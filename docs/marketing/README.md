## How to Use This Playbook

### If You're The Founder (Solo)

**Weeks 1-4:** Execute `00_IMMEDIATE_ACTION_PLAN.md`
- This is your daily task list for first 30 days
- 7 hours/day focused marketing execution
- Do NOT skip days

**Month 2-3:** Iterate on what's working
- Use `01_kpi_dashboard.md` to identify winning channels
- Double down on high-ROI tactics
- Cut or delegate low-ROI tasks

**Month 4-6:** Seed fundraising
- By now you should have traction (metrics)
- Use press releases and pitch deck templates
- Target: Close $1-3M Seed by Month 6-9

**Month 7-18:** Scale to Series A
- Hire marketing person (Month 9-12)
- Focus on product + customers
- Let team execute marketing playbook
- Series A by Month 15-18

### If You're a Team Member

**Your role:**
- Execute 1-2 channels (e.g., "I own Twitter + Medium")
- Report metrics weekly
- Propose experiments
- Free up founder for strategic work

**Don't:**
- Reinvent the wheel (follow the playbooks)
- Operate in silo (coordinate with team)
- Ignore metrics (what gets measured gets managed)

### If You're a Marketing Hire (Post-Seed)

**First 30 days:**
- Read entire playbook (all docs)
- Identify gaps (what's missing?)
- Propose improvements
- Execute quick wins

**First 90 days:**
- Own 1 pillar (e.g., Community or Content)
- Hit your KPI targets
- Build systems/processes
- Report to founder weekly

## FAQs

### Q: Do I really need to spend 7 hours/day on marketing?
**A:** In the first 3 months, yes. After that, delegate.

Sakana AI's David Ha was extremely active on Twitter during their launch. That network became their customers.

### Q: What if I'm not a good writer?
**A:** Use the templates. Hire a freelance editor on Upwork ($50/article) to polish your drafts.

### Q: Can I skip some channels?
**A:** Yes. Pick 2-3 core channels and execute them well. Better to own Twitter + Medium than to be mediocre at 10 channels.

### Q: What if I don't get press coverage?
**A:** Keep building community and traction. Press follows momentum. Get to 5,000 GitHub stars and journalists will find you.

### Q: Should I hire a PR agency?
**A:** Not until Series A. Bootstrap with direct outreach first. Agencies cost $5K-10K/month and have mixed results for early-stage startups.

### Q: What's the ONE most important thing?
**A:** **Consistency**. Post daily. Engage daily. Build daily. For 90 days straight.
