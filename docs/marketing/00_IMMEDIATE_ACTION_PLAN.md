## Time Management Tips

### Protect Your Time
- **Block 7 hours/day** for marketing (don't let coding steal this time)
- **9am-12pm**: Deep work (writing, strategy)
- **1pm-4pm**: Execution (posting, emailing, building)
- **4pm-6pm**: Engagement (replies, calls, community)

### Avoid These Traps
1. **Perfectionism**: Done > perfect (iterate fast)
2. **Rabbit holes**: Set timers, stick to plan
3. **Reactive mode**: Check Twitter/email 3Ã— daily max, not constantly
4. **Analysis paralysis**: Make decision in 10 mins or less

### Maintain Momentum
- **Daily wins**: Celebrate small progress
- **Weekly review**: What's working? Double down.
- **Monthly reset**: Prevent burnout, adjust strategy
